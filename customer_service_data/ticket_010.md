# Ticket #010:  Unable to Access Cloud Storage - Account: jdoe@example.com

**Customer:** John Doe (jdoe@example.com)
**Product:**  CloudSync Pro

**Reported Problem:**

Customer reports being unable to access their CloudSync Pro account.  They receive the error message "Error Code: CS-403: Unauthorized Access" when attempting to log in via the web application and the desktop client. They've tried multiple times using their password and have confirmed the password is correct.  They state they last accessed their account yesterday without issue.  They are concerned about data loss.

**Resolution Process:**

* **Step 1:** Verified customer's email address and account information.
* **Step 2:** Checked CloudSync Pro server logs for any unusual activity related to jdoe@example.com.  No unusual login attempts were detected.
* **Step 3:**  Initiated a password reset for the account via email.
* **Step 4:**  Customer confirmed receipt of password reset email and successfully logged in using the new password.
* **Step 5:**  Confirmed account access and functionality.  Customer reported all files and folders are accessible.
* **Step 6:**  Advised the customer on best practices for password security, including the use of strong, unique passwords.

**Final Outcome:**

Issue resolved. Customer successfully regained access to their CloudSync Pro account.  No data loss occurred.  The problem was likely a cached credential issue on the customer's end, resolved by the password reset.
