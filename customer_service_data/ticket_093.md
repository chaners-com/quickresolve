# Ticket #093:  "CloudSync Pro" - Unable to Sync Files to Server

**Customer:** John Doe (john.doe@email.com)

**Date Submitted:** 2024-10-27

**Product:** CloudSync Pro (Version 3.2.1)

**Problem Reported:**

Customer reports being unable to sync files to the CloudSync Pro server.  He receives the error message: "Connection Failed: Error Code 102 - Server Unreachable." He has confirmed his internet connection is working and has restarted his computer and router. He's tried syncing several different file types (documents, images) with the same result.

**Resolution Steps:**

* **Step 1:** Verified customer's CloudSync Pro subscription status. Subscription is active.
* **Step 2:** Checked server status.  No reported outages.
* **Step 3:**  Requested customer to check their firewall settings to ensure CloudSync Pro is allowed outbound connections on ports 80 and 443.
* **Step 4:**  Customer reported that their firewall was blocking port 443.  He has adjusted the settings to allow CloudSync Pro access.
* **Step 5:**  Asked the customer to attempt a sync again.

**Final Outcome:**

Following the adjustment of firewall settings, the customer successfully synced his files to the CloudSync Pro server.  The issue was resolved.  The customer expressed satisfaction with the resolution.
