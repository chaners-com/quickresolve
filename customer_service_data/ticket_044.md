# Ticket #044:  "Unable to Access Cloud Storage - Error Code: CS-403"

**Customer:** John Doe (john.doe@email.com)
**Product:**  CloudSync Pro (Version 2.3.1)
**Date Submitted:** October 26, 2023

**Reported Problem:**

Mr. Doe reports he is unable to access his CloudSync Pro cloud storage. He receives the error message "Error Code: CS-403: Access Denied" when attempting to log in via the desktop application.  He states he has confirmed his username and password multiple times and hasn't changed them recently. He also mentions he can access the CloudSync Pro website and his account settings without issue.


**Resolution Process:**

* **Step 1:**  Confirmed user credentials with Mr. Doe. Confirmed they matched the account details.
* **Step 2:**  Requested Mr. Doe check his system's date and time settings, ensuring they are accurate.  He confirmed this.
* **Step 3:**  Asked Mr. Doe to try restarting his computer. He did so, but the issue persisted.
* **Step 4:**  Suggested Mr. Doe uninstall and reinstall the CloudSync Pro desktop application.  He followed these steps.
* **Step 5:**  After reinstallation, Mr. Doe successfully accessed his CloudSync Pro storage.

**Final Outcome:**

The issue was resolved by reinstalling the CloudSync Pro desktop application.  The initial error was likely caused by a corrupted installation file or local data cache conflict.
